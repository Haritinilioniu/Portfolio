Wooden Blocks

This piece is dedicated to childhood. A place where games happen without a "desired outcome" or a "business plan". It's the act of playing just for the sake of playing. A human need often dismissed and forgotten, but a very important one. A process of discovering and learning that requires only curiosity as a teacher. The cold, sharp and heavy metal "wooden" blocks mirror the games we gradually forgot to play, we started to fear playing, as it was too risky. Too prone to failure.
